For one week, every time money leaves your hands, write down:

  1. What you bought - The actual item or service
  2. How much you spent - The exact amount
  3. What you felt before, during, and after - Your emotional state

Don't judge it. Don't try to change it. Just witness it. You might discover you only overspend when lonely, or that guilt is your most common spending companion.
